Detailed Description

Meshes are simply collections of surfaces, vertex data, and index data. This data is offloaded into separate buffers (the VBO and IBO using them) and deleted from RAM completely. Loading may take a significant amount of time, based on the size of the mesh and the amount of surface merging that must take place.
